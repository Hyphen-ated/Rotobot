Rules:
* Played on cockatrice by default, but if both players agree they can use whatever program they want.
* 40 card decks. Round robin matches.
* Draft-matters cards don't work.
* All cards with the supertype "Basic" are free.
* After the draft you publish your maindeck and are locked in for each game 1. Don't look at other maindecks until you decide on yours.
* There's 46 rounds in the draft.
* Sets/products that are fully spoiled when the draft begins are legal if someone points out that they exist before the end of the first draft round.
